# ReliefNet Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: October 30, 2025  
**Last Updated**: October 30, 2025

## Introduction

ReliefNet ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and telemedicine services.

By using ReliefNet, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

**For Patients:**
- Full name, email address, phone number
- Date of birth, gender
- Profile photograph (optional)
- Medical history and health information
- Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)

**For Healthcare Professionals:**
- Full name, email address, phone number
- Medical license/ID number
- Specialization, qualifications, and experience
- Professional profile information
- Bank account details for payments (if applicable)

### 2. Technical Information

- Device information (model, OS version, unique identifiers)
- IP address and location data
- App usage statistics and analytics
- Log files and crash reports
- Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device tokens for push notifications

### 3. Communication Data

- Text messages exchanged via in-app chat
- Video and audio call metadata (duration, participants, timestamps)
- Call quality metrics
- Session notes and appointment records

### 4. Payment Information

- Transaction history
- Payment method details (processed by PhonePe, Google Pay)
- Billing information

**Note**: We do not store complete credit/debit card numbers. Payment processing is handled by PCI-DSS compliant third-party providers.

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses

1. **Service Provision**
   - Facilitate appointments between patients and doctors
   - Enable video/audio consultations via WebRTC
   - Process payments securely
   - Send appointment confirmations and reminders

2. **Communication**
   - Deliver push notifications for bookings and messages
   - Send OTP verification codes via email
   - Provide customer support
   - Send important updates about our services

3. **Platform Improvement**
   - Analyze app usage to improve user experience
   - Monitor and fix technical issues
   - Develop new features based on user needs
   - Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

4. **Legal Compliance**
   - Comply with applicable healthcare regulations
   - Respond to legal requests and prevent harm
   - Enforce our Terms of Service

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Share Information With:

1. **Healthcare Providers**
   - Patient information is shared with booked doctors for consultation purposes
   - Doctors can view patient profiles and appointment details

1. **Service Providers**
   - **Railway**: Cloud hosting and infrastructure
   - **MongoDB Atlas**: Database services
   - **Firebase**: Push notifications and analytics
   - **SendGrid**: Email delivery (OTP, notifications)
   - **PhonePe/Google Pay**: Payment processing

3. **Legal Requirements**
   - When required by law, court order, or government regulation
   - To protect our rights, safety, or property
   - In case of suspected fraud or security threats

### We Do NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your health data for marketing purposes
- Disclose your information without your consent (except as legally required)

## Data Security

### Security Measures

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted between app and server is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Authentication**: JWT-based secure session management
- **Access Control**: Role-based access with strict permissions
- **Secure Storage**: Encrypted database storage on MongoDB Atlas
- **WebRTC**: Peer-to-peer encrypted video/audio streams
- **Regular Audits**: Periodic security assessments and updates

### Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Data may be retained for up to 2 years after last activity
- **Legal Requirements**: Some data retained longer for legal/regulatory compliance
- **Deletion Requests**: You may request account and data deletion at any time

## Your Privacy Rights

### You Have the Right To:

1. **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Correction**: Update or correct inaccurate information
3. **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
4. **Portability**: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
5. **Opt-Out**: Disable non-essential notifications and marketing communications
6. **Withdraw Consent**: Revoke permissions previously granted
